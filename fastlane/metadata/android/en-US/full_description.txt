HappyTaxes is a private, offline-first cash-basis bookkeeping app with receipt OCR. Made for freelancers, sole traders, small business, even personal finance. Track your finances, prepare for tax time, and keep your data yours.

<b>KEY FEATURES</b>

• Track income and expenses with receipts
• Generate compliant Profit & Loss reports
• Maintain 6+ years of records
• Receipt capture with OCR (Tesseract - on-device only)
• Local backup & restore (ZIP export/import)
• Soft delete with 30-day recovery
• Work completely offline - no internet required
• 100% private - your data never leaves your device

<b>PRIVACY FIRST</b>

• No data collection
• No tracking or analytics
• No ads
• No cloud sync
• All data stored locally on your device
• You have complete control over your data

<b>PERFECT FOR</b>

• Online sellers tracking income and costs
• Freelancers managing business finances
• Small businesses needing simple bookkeeping
• Personal expense tracking and budgeting
• Anyone preparing for tax time
• People who value privacy and offline functionality
