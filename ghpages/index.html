HappyTaxes

Proper bookkeeping. Private. Free.

HappyTaxes is a cash basis bookkeeping app for tracking income and expenses. Whether you're managing personal finances, freelancing, or running a small business, HappyTaxes helps you keep receipts organized, generate compliant Profit & Loss reports, and maintain records for tax time.

Key Features

  • Track income and expenses with receipts
  • Generate compliant Profit and Loss reports
  • Maintain 6+ years of records
  • Receipt capture with OCR (Tesseract - on-device only)
  • Local backup and restore (ZIP export/import)
  • Soft delete with 30-day recovery
  • Work completely offline

100% Privacy Guaranteed

No data is collected. All information you enter (receipts, categories, amounts, images, transactions) is stored exclusively on your device.
